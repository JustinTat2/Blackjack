Every png of a playing card is labelled "card_{x}" where x is between 1 and 52.

For 1 <= x <= 13, the cards are in the Clubs suit, in ascending order.

For 14 <= x <= 26, the cards are in the Hearts suit, in ascending order.

For 27 <= x <= 39, the cards are in the Spades suit, in ascending order.

For 40 <= x <= 52, the cards are in the Diamonds suit, in ascending order.
